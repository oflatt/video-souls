How to Play

The tutorial level is the best way to learn the rhythm, but here’s a quick refresher before you dive in.

  • k Parry incoming blows. Hit it just before the strike lands to block all damage.
  • w a s d Hold a direction while parrying to deflect to that side. Let go for a neutral parry.
  • j Strike back when you see an opening to keep the pressure on.
  • Early parries Reduce the damage but don’t fully block it—you’ll still take a sliver. Aim for perfect timing.
  • Attack indicator Watch the glowing arrow to learn which direction to parry.
  • x An X in the center means hold no direction—parry straight ahead.
