Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 17, 2025

Overview

Video Souls is a rhythm-combat experience built by Oliver Flatt. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the game or visit its supporting websites, how that information is used, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Gameplay data (local only): Saved levels and progress are stored locally on your device. They are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate engagement, such as page views, device types, and geographic regions. Google Analytics may set cookies or collect device identifiers.
  • Embedded content: Video Souls integrates YouTube content. When videos load, YouTube may collect usage data under its own policies.

How We Use Information

  • Improve the game and website experience.
  • Monitor aggregate usage and performance.
  • Detect and fix potential errors.

Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third parties to deliver certain functionality:

  • Google Analytics: Provides usage insights. See Google’s Privacy Policy for details.
  • YouTube: Powers in-game video playback. Refer to Google’s Product Privacy Guide.

Your use of these services is subject to their respective terms.

Your Choices

  • You can clear local save data through your browser or system settings.
  • You may opt out of Google Analytics using the official browser add-on.
  • You can disable cookies within your browser, though some features may not function fully.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, email oflatt@gmail.com.
