How to Play

The in-game tutorial is the best teacher, but here are the essentials so you can practice the timing.
  • KParry incoming blows. You want to press right before the attack lands.
  • WASDHold a direction while parrying to perform a directional parry.
  • JStrike back when you see an opening to keep the pressure on.
  • Early parriesstill reduce damage but you’ll bleed a portion through, so aim for the exact moment.
  • Attack indicatorsThe glowing arrow telegraphs the direction of the hit. Directional parry in the opposite direction! An X indicator means a center parry, so release all directions to parry it.
